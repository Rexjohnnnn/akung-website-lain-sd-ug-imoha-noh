Have Questions? We Have Answers!

Check out our frequently asked questions to find the information you need.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, Gcash and bank transfers. You can choose the most convenient option for you at checkout.
How can I track my order?
Tracking your order is easy! Once your order is shipped, we'll send you a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly with the courier.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy, we offer refunds for eligible items within a specified return period. Please check our refund policy for more details.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Yes, you can make changes or cancel your order within a short period after placing it. However, once the order is processed and shipped, changes may not be possible. Feel free to contact our friendly customer support team for assistance.
How can I contact customer support?
We're here to help! You can reach our dedicated customer support team via email, phone, or live chat. Whether you have questions, concerns, or need assistance, we're always just a message away.
